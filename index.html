Vision

CFRCSR Integrated Skill Development formed to lead the rapid integrated growth of various sectors of the economy and determining priorities through projects, plans and other initiatives of the government and private sectors for the most effective and balanced utilisation of resources and which directly enhance the standard of living of the people, increasing production and offering opportunities to all for employment in the services of the community and establish as a prominent player by creating a unique platform for providing end to end solutions in this field. The Company aims to coordinate and harmonize the approach to skill development/up-skilling among various Central Ministries/Departments, State Governments, National Skill Development Corporation, other Government organisations the non-Government organisation, and the private sector.
